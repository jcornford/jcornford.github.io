Izhikevich Model Neuron

The four parameters: a, b, c and d have different roles. The parameter "a" represents the recovery of the membrane after the spike, the parameter "b" takes into account the sensibility of the neuron to the fluctuation of the voltage, the parameter "c" is used to set the maximum amplitude of the spike after which the neuron's voltage is reset and the parameter "d" determines the after-spike overshoot reset. http://www.izhikevich.org/publications/whichmod.pdf
